About DAY 1 - Personal Fitness Tracker

DAY 1 - Personal Fitness Tracker is a dedicated fitness tracking application designed to help you organize your training data, visualize your progress, and stay committed to your health goals.

Key Features

  • Workout Logging: Record your duration, date, and calories burned.
  • Exercise Tracking: Log specific sets, reps, and weights for every movement.
  • Category Management: Organize exercises by muscle groups like Chest, Back, or Legs.
  • Personal History: Access a detailed history of your past sessions to track strength gains.
